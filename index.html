Aberdeen Geothermal Feasibility Project Consortium Overview

The Consortium brings together partners with a common goal in delivering geothermal contibtuions to the energy and heating mix in Aberdeen, across the UK and the world.
Our mission is to collaborate to de-risk and inform future geothermal delivery projects through impactful science, innovation, and feasibility projects.

Consortium Partners, Stakeholders, and Supporters

  • University of Aberdeen
  • NHS Grampian
  • Aberdeen City Council
  • Aberdeen Heat and Power
  • Robert Gordon University
  • Aberdeen Renewable Energy Group
  • National Geothermal Centre
  • University of Leeds (Campus Geothermal Project)
  • British Geological Survey
  • TownRock Energy Ltd.
  • Primeval Energy Ltd.
  • CeraPhi Energy Ltd.
  • Reconnoitre Ltd.
