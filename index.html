Consortium Overview

The Consortium brings together partners with a common goal in delivering geothermal to the energy and heating mix in Aberdeen, across the UK and the world. Our mission is to collaborate to de-risk and inform future geothermal delivery projects through impactful science, innovation, education, training, and feasibility assessments.
